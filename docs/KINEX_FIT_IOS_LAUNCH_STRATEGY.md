# Kinex Fit iOS Launch Strategy
## Launch Weekend: March 14-15, 2026

---

## 📱 Positioning & Messaging

### Core Narrative
**"Kinex Fit: Official iOS Launch — Out of Beta"**

After months of beta testing and refinement with our community, Kinex Fit is graduating from beta and launching officially on iOS.

### Key Messages
- **Journey Story:** Beta testing → User feedback → Refinement → Official launch
- **Community Focus:** Built with and for real athletes
- **Value Proposition:** Smart training, not cheap training
- **iOS Milestone:** Native app experience, polished and ready

### Constraints
- ❌ **Avoid:** "Saving money on trainers" (respects gym/trainer relationships)
- ❌ **Avoid:** Positioning as trainer replacement
- ✅ **Focus:** Smarter training, adaptive workouts, community-driven development

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

### Primary
- Fitness enthusiasts who discover workouts on social media (Instagram/TikTok)
- People who want structure but don't want to pay for personal training
- Gym-goers who feel stuck in routines or plateaus

### Secondary  
- HYROX/competitive athletes (existing community)
- Home workout enthusiasts
- People tracking fitness progress
